Query Selectors:
    + Comparison:
        - $eq (equal), $gt (greater), $gte (greater + equal), $in (in)
        - $lt (less), $lte (less + equal), $ne (not equal), $nin (not in)
    + Logical:
        - $and, $not, $nor, $or
    + Element:
        - $exists, $type
    + Evaluation:
        - $expr (expression), $joinSchema, $regex (regular expression)
        - $text, $where
    + Array:
        - $all, $elemMatch, $size
    + Bitwise:
        - $bitsAllClear (0000..00), $bitsAllSet (111...11)
        - $bitsAnyClear (any has 0), $bitsAnySet (any has 1)
Projection Operators: $, $elemMatch, $meta, $slice ???
Miscellaneous Operators: $comment, $rand
